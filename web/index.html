Engage: Our philosophy.

Our audience

This is the currency we deal in. What makes our audience unique is its DNA. We engage with thousands of active jobseekers every week. But what’s different is our thousands of passive jobseekers; readers who don’t realise they’re your perfect candidate until we connect them with you.

Our scale

We don’t mean to boast, but we're huge - in fact no other regional media brand comes close. We have literally thousands of newspapers, magazines and websites. That means more eyeballs, more candidates and more ways to find the right person for the job.

Our method

Our bespoke blend of print, online and social media, our creative solutions attract the right people into the right businesses. And don’t forget: we're recruitment people - it's in our blood. We look after everyone, from one-man-bands to blue chip companies. We know the jobs market and we know how to deliver.

Our guarantee

We do things differently but we always seal the deal. We don’t just take a booking and hope for the best. We guarantee to advertise every vacancy until we find the perfect person for the job.

Our pricing

Our recruitment platforms are surprisingly affordable and coupled with our ‘run til filled’ guarantee, it’s among the most cost effective methods of recruitment out there.
